# **Appendix D – Intellectual Property Plan**

## **D.1 Overview**
- Purpose of the IP Plan in supporting technology protection and commercialization
- Scope: trademarks, copyrights, patents, and licensing arrangements between entities
- Objectives: protect proprietary assets, ensure brand integrity, and define revenue entitlements

---

## **D.2 Ownership Structure**
| Asset Category | Registered / Pending Owner | Description | Notes |
|----------------|----------------------------|--------------|-------|
| Core source code, APIs, SDKs | **3neti R&D OPC** | Foundational software modules for voucher issuance, redemption, and programmable disbursement | Covered under registered copyright |
| Brand name, logos, and marks | **x-Change Philippines Inc.** (licensed use) | Trademarks for public-facing operations | Owned by 3neti R&D OPC, licensed exclusively to x-Change PH |
| Infrastructure scripts, templates, and models | **3neti R&D OPC** | Deployment automation, AI models, and data templates | Proprietary assets under confidentiality agreements |
| Documentation and design materials | Shared | Manuals, technical docs, and marketing creatives | Subject to mutual IP acknowledgment |

---

## **D.3 Trademark Strategy**
- Filing of “x-Change,” “Voucher PH,” and related marks under IPOPHL
- Coverage: wordmark and logo, classes 9 (software), 36 (financial services), 42 (SaaS)
- Regional and international filings via Madrid Protocol (Phase 2)
- Brand-usage guidelines and co-branding rules between 3neti R&D and licensees

---

## **D.4 Copyright & Patent Protection**
- Registration of software source code and documentation with the **National Library / IPO Philippines**
- Pending patent applications:
    - *Voucher Tokenization Process*
    - *Programmable Disbursement Framework*
    - *Dynamic Redemption Routing Engine*
- Periodic IP audits and code escrow arrangements for investor confidence

---

## **D.5 Licensing & Commercialization Framework**
| Party | Role | Rights | Obligations |
|--------|------|---------|--------------|
| **3neti R&D OPC** | IP Owner / Licensor | Grants exclusive Philippine operating license | Maintains R&D, updates, and patents |
| **x-Change Philippines Inc.** | Licensee / Operator | Uses IP for commercial deployment | Pays royalties / license fees; ensures compliance |
| **Partners (Banks, EMIs, Enterprises)** | Sub-licensees / Integrators | Limited use via API / SDK agreements | Adhere to usage, branding, and data-security clauses |

---

## **D.6 Revenue & Royalty Model**
- Annual licensing fee structure between entities
- Royalty percentage (e.g., 5 % of gross transactional revenue) remitted to 3neti R&D OPC
- Mechanism for technology-upgrade cost recovery and reinvestment in R&D

---

## **D.7 Governance & Change Control**
- Joint IP Management Committee for oversight
- Approval workflow for new trademarks, patents, or derivative works
- Procedures for amendments, sublicensing, and termination

---

## **D.8 Risk Mitigation & Compliance**
- NDA and assignment clauses for developers and partners
- Source-code escrow and version-control access policies
- IP insurance and enforcement roadmap against infringement

---

## **D.9 Future IP Development Roadmap**
- Planned R&D domains: blockchain anchoring, facial KYC modules, and open-API compliance tooling
- Technology-transfer provisions for cross-border licensing (e.g., Singapore and ASEAN markets)
