# **Appendix H – Key Personnel Incentive Plan**
*Framework for executive and employee incentives for x-Change Philippines, Inc.*

---

## H.1 Purpose and Philosophy

The **Key Personnel Incentive Plan (KPIP)** aligns the interests of leadership and employees with the long-term growth of **x-Change Philippines, Inc.**  
Its purpose is to attract, retain, and motivate individuals who will drive operational excellence, product innovation, and market expansion, while preserving **3neti R&D OPC’s** intellectual property and ensuring **investor value creation**.

---

## H.2 Incentive Structure Overview

| Component | Description | Vesting / Term | Notes |
|------------|--------------|----------------|-------|
| **Base Salary** | Competitive compensation aligned with fintech market benchmarks. | Annual | ₱250k–₱400k/month for senior execs (CEO/COO) |

| **Fringe Benefits** | Health insurance, car plan, training, and allowances. | Ongoing | Reviewed annually |

## H.4 Vesting and Acceleration

- **Standard Vesting:** 4 years with 1-year cliff. After the first year, 25% vests, with the remainder vesting monthly or quarterly thereafter.
- **Performance Accelerators:** Early vesting of up to 25–50% may occur upon hitting specific milestones, such as:
    - Achieving ₱X million in GMV or revenue,
    - Securing 3+ major institutional partners (banks, EMIs),
    - Achieving profitability (EBITDA positive).
- **Change of Control:** Unvested shares may accelerate up to 50% upon a sale or merger event.
- **Termination:** Unvested options lapse automatically; vested options may be exercised within 90 days post-termination unless for cause.

## H.6 Retention and Exit Incentives

- **Retention Bonus:** One-time bonus equivalent to 6–12 months’ salary, payable upon:
    - Successful completion of Series B raise, or
    - Company reaching defined revenue threshold (e.g., ₱100M annualized GMV).
- **Exit Incentive:** For key executives with ≥2 years tenure, an additional 0.5–1.0% equity-equivalent payout at liquidity event (IPO, acquisition).
- **Non-Compete and IP Assignment:** Mandatory for all ESOP grantees; ensures continued protection of 3neti R&D OPC’s intellectual property.

---

## H.7 Governance and Administration

1. **Administration:** The plan is administered by the **Compensation & ESOP Committee** under the authority of the Board.
2. **Documentation:** Each participant signs an **Option Grant Agreement** and **Confidentiality & IP Assignment** agreement.
3. **Amendments:** Any modification to pool size, vesting terms, or key grants requires **Board approval** and **investor consent** under Reserved Matters (§G.4.3).
4. **Valuation:** Strike price set at fair market value determined by the most recent independent valuation or financing round.
5. **Accounting:** Option expense recognized per applicable Philippine Financial Reporting Standards (PFRS 2).

## H.10 Sample Clause – Option Grant Agreement

> “The Optionee’s right to exercise the Options shall vest in accordance with the Schedule attached hereto.

> **Non-Compete and IP Ownership:**  
> “The Optionee acknowledges that all inventions, source code, and documentation created in the course of employment shall belong exclusively to x-Change Philippines, Inc. or its Licensor (3neti R&D OPC). The Optionee shall not engage in any competing business for a period of 12 months following termination.”
