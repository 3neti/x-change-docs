## **Investor Protection Provisions**

- **Anti-Dilution:** Standard weighted-average protection for future issuances below post-money valuation.
- **Right of First Refusal:** Applies to any share transfer prior to Series B or liquidity event.
- **Drag-Along / Tag-Along:** Ensures aligned exit mechanisms for majority and minority holders.
- **Lock-Up Period:** Founders subject to 3-year lock-up with 12-month cliff and linear vesting for employee options (Appendix H).
- **Performance Milestones:** Release of founder carried equity and employee options tied to commercial launch and break-even targets.
- **Audit and Compliance:** Mandatory annual external audit; regulatory reporting in line with BSP and AMLC guidelines.

---

## **Exit and Liquidity Options**

1. **Strategic Acquisition (Preferred Path):** Sale to bank, EMI, or regional fintech within 5 years at 8–10× EBITDA multiple.
2. **Series B Round:** Secondary liquidity event once EBITDA > ₱100 M or revenues > ₱200 M.
3. **Dividend Distribution:** Available post-Year 4 if cash reserves > 12-month OPEX.
4. **Management Buyback or Public Listing:** Optional investor exit after Year 5.
