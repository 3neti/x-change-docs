# x-Change - Information Memorandum

**x-Change** is a programmable digital voucher system that flips traditional money transfer on its head—by letting the *recipient* decide where and how to redeem funds.

Instead of pushing money into a specific bank or wallet account, we **escrow the funds and issue a QR or SMS-based voucher code**. The recipient then pulls the funds into **any preferred destination**—bank, e-wallet, or cash-out agent—on their terms, at their time.

We're not another wallet. We're building **programmable, account-agnostic digital cash** that's:

- App-less for recipients
- KYC/AML compliant for issuers
- Secure, traceable, and auditable by design

This is ideal for:
- **Small loan disbursements**
- **Money changer and remittance OTC disbursements**
- **Social welfare and disaster relief (dole-outs)**
- **Tipping service workers** (waiters, caddies, cleaners, etc.)
- **Disbursements** (aid, rebates, wages, incentives) when full banking info is not available
- **Informal economy payments**
- **Offline-first or print-based money delivery**
- **Gifting** without needing the recipient's account details

> ⚖️ **Regulatory note:** x-Change is a **technology infrastructure provider**, not a financial institution. We operate **under the license perimeter** of our EMI and banking partners. We do not custody funds or transmit money directly.
